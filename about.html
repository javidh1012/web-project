About Us

Student Internship Portal is a simple and student-friendly platform created to connect students with internship opportunities across various industries. We offer a curated list of internships, a smooth application process with resume uploads, and a completely free experience for all users. Our mission is to empower students through practical, real-world experience and help them kickstart their careers. The portal is open to college students, companies offering internships, and even faculty coordinators in the future. Built using HTML, CSS, PHP, MySQL, and Bootstrap, the platform runs locally via XAMPP for easy development and access.
